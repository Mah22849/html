My Memorable Day
My first day at school is. one of the most memorable events in my life, It was Thursday 5 January 2005. With many unknown fears, I accompanied my mother to school. As she left me, I realized that I was in an unfamiliar world. Then one senior student guided me to my classroom. But my heart sank I noticed tears trickling down the cheeks of most of the girls. However, our class teacher’s kind and friendly words comforted us. Soon I joined the assembly. The principal delivered a short speech welcoming us. Then we were back in the classroom. Then our class teacher told us about various things. After some time the first bell went. Then new teachers came in each of the following periods. They were very friendly too. After the Tiffin period, we received our English teacher. She told us a number of stories. To me, her class was the most entertaining. At last the final bell went. Then I accompanied my mother back home. The sweet memories of the day will remain alive in my heart until the last day of my life.
